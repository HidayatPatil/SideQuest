    Our Logo

    Our logo features our charming mascots — a clever fox and a cute derpy frog. Our mascots appear frequently throughout our app and symbolizes friendship and adventure. By using mascots in our logo, we also hope to portray our app as fun and welcoming.

    Our wordmark uses the Darumadrop One font, which is inspired by Japanese handwriting combined with the classic Japanese game "Daruma Otoshi". We chose this font because it gives a playful and child-like vibe, perfectly fitting the overall theme of our app.

    Typography

    We chose Rubik for its clean, modern look that balances playfulness with readability. Its rounded, friendly design reflects the approachable and fun nature of our app, while maintaining clarity for users on their journey through quests.

    Why Everyday Adventure Matter?

    The Power of Shared Experiences

    Section 1

    Engaging in local social activities offers numerous benefits, especially for busy professionals, newcomers to a city, and introverts seeking meaningful connections. Participating in group-oriented hobbies, such as joining a book club or attending community events, can help individuals meet like-minded people and establish a sense of belonging. For instance, silent book clubs have gained popularity by providing a low-pressure environment where participants can enjoy reading alongside others without the expectation of conversation.

    Additionally, joining professional networking groups can enhance career prospects by building trust and credibility within one's industry. Regular interactions in such groups often lead to valuable connections and even friendships, contributing to both professional and personal growth.

    Section 2

    For introverts or those new to an area, finding and engaging in activities that align with personal interests can be an effective strategy for making new friends. Consistently attending recurring events or groups allows for gradual relationship building in comfortable settings.

    Overall, actively seeking out and participating in local activities not only enriches one's social life but also contributes to professional development and a deeper connection with the community.

    Color Palette

    Our color palette balances playful energy with functionality, designed to make users feel welcome, excited, and ready to explore. It’s bright, accessible, and full of personality, drawn directly from our mascots, evoking a sense of bold adventure and curiosity.

    We chose blue for our primary color, as it’s easier to see compared to colors like orange or green. It is universally appealing and commonly associated with trustworthiness, stability, and a sense of community. Blue also offers strong contrast on digital interfaces, which enhances readability and accessibility for all users. This choice supports an inclusive design that prioritizes both visual comfort and clarity.

    All of our colors meet at least the WCAG AA standard for contrast, ensuring text remains legible against its background. Through out our design majority of elements meet the AAA standard, the few that don’t are only used as a secondary element, where it is not the focus and visibility is improved by using bold text.

    Visuals

    Our visuals are all about creating a modern, approachable, and playful experience. Every element is designed with soft, rounded edges and plenty of breathing room to make the app feel inviting and easy to navigate.

    Graphics

    Fox + Frog mascots: Our graphics are a cute, colorful blend of hand-drawn adventure scenes. Each image feels like a scene from a rpg where real life is the adventure. Whether it’s onboarding screens, or our logo, the graphics invite users into a world where even a coffee run can feel like a grand expedition!. Our playful duo are both animals that appear frequently in fantasy and adventure.

    Scout the fox takes inspiration from the story “The Little Prince” ,the fox teaches the Little Prince the importance of creating meaningful connections and the importance of relationships. That understanding and appreciating others require investing time and effort in each other. Our fox and his friend are the perfect representations of the message and vision we wish to share with SideQuest.
