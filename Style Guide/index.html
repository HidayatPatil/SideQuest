    VISION

    SideQuest — where solo plans become shared experiences. From a quick shopping trip, a impromptu hike, or a concert you don’t want to go alone. There’s always someone to join you on your quest.

    PURPOSE

    Our purpose of connecting people again in the real world. Sharing everyday moments and meeting new people through spontaneous expeditions. More of our lives are moving online, we wanted to take a break from the main story and bring back the fun with adventures.

    CONNECTION

    At the core of every quest is the power of human connection. Whether you’re new in town, an introvert seeking company, or just someone craving shared experiences, our app helps you find your people!

    Our Logo

    Our logo features a charming duo: a clever fox and a delightfully derpy frog, both geared up as tiny adventurers. Together, they symbolize curiosity, courage, and camaraderie—the perfect mascots for our users’ everyday quests.

    Section 1 - Rubik

    Rubik is a sans serif font family with slightly rounded corners designed by Philipp Hubert and Sebastian Fischer at Hubert & Fischer

    We chose Rubik for its clean, modern look that balances playfulness with readability. Its sans-serif, rounded yet bold design reflects the approachable and fun nature of our app, while still maintaining clarity and ease of use for users on their journey through quests. Rubik’s versatility makes it an ideal choice for digital experiences that aim to be both engaging and accessible.

    Section 2 - OpenDyslexic

    OpenDyslexic is a free, open-source typeface designed to improve readability for individuals with dyslexia. With features like a weighted bottom and uniquely shaped letters, it enhances readability and reduces letter confusion. Its thoughtful design makes it helpful not only for users with dyslexia, but also for those with ADHD or other reading difficulties.

    Visuals

    Our visuals are all about creating a modern, approachable, and playful experience. Every element is designed with soft, rounded edges and plenty of breathing room to make the app feel inviting and easy to navigate.

    Graphics

    Our graphics are a cute, colorful blend of hand-drawn adventure scenes. Each image feels like a scene from a rpg where real life is the adventure. Whether it’s onboarding screens, or our logo, the graphics invite users into a world where even a coffee run can feel like a grand expedition!. Our playful duo are both animals that appear frequently in fantasy and adventure games.

    Scout the fox takes inspiration from the story “The Little Prince” ,the fox teaches the Little Prince the importance of creating meaningful connections and the importance of relationships. That understanding and appreciating others require investing time and effort in each other. Our fox and his friend are the perfect representations of the message and vision we wish to share with SideQuest.
